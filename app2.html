Warehouse Management System(Click enter APP)

This project is small but very practical. It can help people like me who are looking for a job by describing their education, work experience, and skills. Combined with recruitment requirements, quickly generate a new resume. You can also optimize your resume information according to your requirements on the basis of your input resume.

PLEASE NOTE: Resume generation should be based on facts.

The purpose of developing this project is to show my ability to learn quickly, especially the learning of new platforms, and the calling of API interfaces. In the past jobs, I also have experience in using a series of platforms such as AWS and FireBase.

The development of the project took me less than a day. It uses the gradio framework to create front-end pages through AI. The front-end framework even has front-end and back-end communication and cross-domain solutions embedded, which is very friendly to small projects. After the backend receives the data, it will first optimize the input content through the OpenAI interface, and provide an example, and then send it to OpenAI together with the optimization goal to generate the final resume. The generated resume will include a Markdown version, which is convenient for users to use directly.
